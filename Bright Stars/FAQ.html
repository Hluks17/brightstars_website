Frequently Asked Questions

How can my child join a tutoring session?

Complete the contact form on our Contact page with your child’s details and subject needs. Our team will contact you to arrange times and placement.

Are the tutoring sessions free?

We run a mix of free and subsidised sessions. Please enquire using the contact form for details tailored to your situation.

Can I volunteer to tutor?

Yes. Volunteers with suitable subject knowledge are welcome. Fill the enquiry form on the Contact page and include your experience and availability.

What age groups do you support?

We support learners from primary school through high school, and we run specific workshops aimed at older students preparing for exams.

How do you protect student privacy?

We only collect necessary information to deliver our services and do not share personal data with third parties without consent.
